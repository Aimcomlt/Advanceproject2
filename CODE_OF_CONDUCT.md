# Literary Sovereignty Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to foster an open and welcoming environment. We commit to making participation in the project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of positive behavior:
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness.
- Respecting differing opinions and experiences.
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on community benefit.

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory comments.
- Public or private harassment, including doxxing.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Disruptive trolling or repeated inflammatory remarks.

## Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and take corrective action when violations occur. Maintainers may remove or reject contributions and may ban contributors temporarily or permanently for behaviors they deem inappropriate.

## Scope
This Code applies within project spaces and when individuals represent the project in public channels, events, or digital communities.

## Enforcement
- Report incidents to `security@literarysovereignty.xyz` with relevant details.
- Reports will be reviewed confidentially by the guardian council within 72 hours.
- Enforcement actions may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from community spaces.
